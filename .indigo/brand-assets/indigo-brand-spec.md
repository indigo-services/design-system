# Indigo Brand Spec

Visual direction:
- Professional B2B software brand, not playful consumer branding.
- Strong Indigo ownership without changing the component API surface.
- Clean geometry, calm hierarchy, and high small-size legibility.
- No purple-first Strapi styling in this tranche.

Preferred palette:
- Primary Indigo: `#1F3D8F`
- Secondary Indigo: `#3559B7`
- Light Accent: `#8CB4FF`
- Pale Accent: `#DCE7FF`
- Ink: `#18253A`
- Dark Canvas: `#071222`
- Light Canvas: `#F5F7FF`

Logo guidance:
- Horizontal wordmark with a simple phone-mark icon on the left.
- Preserve good readability inside Storybook sidebar header sizing.
- Transparent background.
- Provide light and dark variants.

Favicon guidance:
- Use the same phone mark as the logo family.
- Keep it circle-based and crisp at tiny sizes.
- Prefer one simple silhouette over ornamental detail.

Symbol guidance:
- Build a distinct Indigo mark, not a recolored Strapi clone.
- Keep it square and simple enough to survive at 16x16 and 32x32.
- Flat vector only.

Cover guidance:
- Use an editorial product-hero composition.
- Show Indigo as a system brand, not a marketing landing page.
- Avoid stock-photo energy and avoid generic gradient-only covers.
